For my [Master's thesis](/projects/master-thesis), I initially proposed to study the emergence of agency and self-organization within a [cellular automata](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cellular_automaton), defining the agent's "brain", in a feedback loop with its environment through a perception-action interface, defining the agent "body". I intended to study this system using one of the currently most powerful frameworks in cognitive sciences, the [free energy principle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Free_energy_principle).
